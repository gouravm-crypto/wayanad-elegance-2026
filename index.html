The Mission Vehicle

18-Seater Tempo Traveller

₹51,600. 1,600 km package. 16 people — 2 spare seats for comfort and luggage. Door to door from Guindy to God's Own Country and back. The overnight journey isn't the obstacle. It's the opening act.

We booked an 18-seater for 16 people — deliberately. Two spare seats mean extra legroom, space for bags, and nobody cramped on a 1,565km round trip.

Route via Coimbatore avoids multi-state permit complications — Salem → Coimbatore → Gudalur → Lakkidi → Wayanad. Smoother at borders, and the Gudalur dhaba at 3:30 AM is a planned ritual.

~1,565 km actual against a 1,600 km package — 35 km buffer still in hand. Confirm per-km overage rate before departure just in case.

The TT journey kit: Bluetooth speaker. Antakshari + Dumb Charades. Everyone's 30-min playlist queued up. Snack basket. Cards / UNO. Neck pillows + blankets for 2–4 AM. The trip starts when the TT rolls. Not when you arrive.
